HardWork Pays

A Poem By Ra-Chel.



There is no shortcut for hard work that lead to effectiveness.
You must stay disciplined because most of the work is behind the scenes
Laziness is a luxurious mindset that I can not afford to own
it takes many years to become an overnight success


Have a goal. Work towards it. Because there is not a thing,
more fulfilling than reaping the fruits of your labour
Hardwork doesnot break bones,
it rather strengthens the body and mind

Nothing comes easy.You want something in life?
Get off your comfort zone and work for it.
It takes many years to become an overnight success,
victory only comes through wear and tear

A lot of hard workers fall short of success because,
they dont understand that working hard only pays off
when, it is channeled on the right plan of action
If you want to clout, work for it


HardWork! HardWork!! HardWork!!!
